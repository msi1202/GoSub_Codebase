How It Works

Simplifying the subleasing process from start to finish.

For Subleasers

1. List Your Space

Fill in the details, upload photos, and set your rent. It’s fast and intuitive.

2. Connect with Tenants

Receive messages from verified students. Chat securely and review profiles.

3. Sign & Get Paid

Complete your digital sublease agreement and receive secure rent payments online.

For Sublessees

1. Create Your Profile

Show your background, university, and internship details. Build trust with hosts.

2. Search & Filter Listings

Find the perfect sublease using our smart filters and dynamic search.

3. Sign & Move In

Digitally sign the agreement and securely pay your rent. Moving in is a breeze!
