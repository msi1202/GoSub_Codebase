Who We Are

GoSub is a student-focused platform that simplifies subleasing. Whether you're moving out for a co-op or arriving for a summer internship, we bridge the gap with secure listings, seamless messaging, and verified payments. Founded by students, for students.

Our Mission

We aim to eliminate double rent and housing stress for college students by streamlining the subleasing experience with modern tools and a supportive community.
